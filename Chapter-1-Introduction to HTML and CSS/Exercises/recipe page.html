Egg roll

Description:Prepare Filling: Cook a mix of finely chopped vegetables and protein with seasonings. Wrap: Place filling on an egg roll wrapper, fold sides towards center, then roll tightly. Fry: Deep fry egg rolls until golden brown and crispy, around 3-4 minutes. Serve: Drain excess oil, let cool briefly, then serve hot with dipping sauce.

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 2

    Ingredients: Egg, , salt, pepper, green chilies, egg roll wrappers, filling, garlic powder, olive oil.

    Quantity: Eggs: 3, Salt: A pinch, Pepper: A pinch, green chillies: 2, egg roll wrappers: 2, garlic powder:2tbs, olive oil: 5tbs.

    Prepare Filling: Sauté finely chopped veggies and protein with seasonings like ginger, and garlic powder until cooked through and flavorful.

    Wrap: Place a spoonful of filling onto an egg roll wrapper, fold bottom corner over filling, fold in sides, then roll tightly, sealing the final corner with water.

    Serve: Drain excess oil on paper towels, allow to cool slightly, then serve hot with your preferred dipping sauce like sweet and sour or soy sauce.

    Enjoy your crispy, savory egg rolls as a delicious appetizer or snack!Prepare Filling: Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté finely chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrots, onions, garlic) until soft. Add protein (ground pork, chicken, or shrimp) and cook until browned. Season with soy sauce, ginger, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Set aside to cool.

    Egg Rolls: Lay an egg roll wrapper on a clean surface in a diamond shape. Place a spoonful of filling onto the center. Fold the bottom corner over the filling. Fold in the sides, then roll tightly, sealing the final corner with water.

    Heat oil in a deep skillet or pot to 350°F (180°C). Carefully place the egg rolls seam side down into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally, about 3-4 minutes per side.
